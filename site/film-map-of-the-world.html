Methodoly

The dataset used to create this map was scraped from the English Wikipedia using the Wikipedia API. The period of collection was from June 19, 2015â€“June 19, 2019.

Inspired and heavily influenced by the People Map of the US by The Pudding.

Dataset

The complete list of films set in each country was gathered from thousands of Wikipedia film pages, including short films and documentary films, under hundreds of "Films set in X" (where X is a country/city/state, etc.) categories and subcategories on Wikipedia. Median daily page view was used to determine top 10 or fewer films. Since one film can be set in various countries, repeating occurrences of films were included on the map.

The unprocessed and final datasets are available on GitHub.
